What is SkillSwap?

SkillSwap is a community-powered learning platform where people can both teach and learn skills through mutual exchange. It empowers users to list what they can teach and what they want to learn, and then intelligently matches them with complementary users. Our mission is to create a collaborative environment that democratizes education, encourages community growth, and makes learning accessible and free of cost.
